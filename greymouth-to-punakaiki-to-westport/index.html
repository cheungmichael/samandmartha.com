Greymouth to Punakaiki to Westport

We left Greymouth and drove up to Punakaiki and did a small walk to see the pancake rocks. They were flat and layered and there were lookouts along the way.

Then we drove to Westport. We did some shopping in town. Then we had tea and then to the the beach. It was called Carters Beach.
