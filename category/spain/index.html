Arc de Triomf and Parc de la Ciutadella

Today we got the metro to the Arc de Triomf. Then we walked down to the park. We played catch and then went to the ping pong tables and played with the tennis ball on the tables. Afterwards we found a small play area and played there for a while. Then we walked back to the metro and got it home.

Picasso Museum

Today we walked around the city centre and went to the Picasso Museum. He painted people in his Blue Period and his Rose Period. He also painted Cubism. It is where you paint things from different angles all on top of each other. Picasso’s faces had eyes and noses and ears at the front.

Sagrada Familia

Today we went to a church called the Sagrada Familia. They are still building it after 130 years. It should be finished in 6 years time. Inside the church we saw beautiful stained glass windows.

Camp Nou

Today we met up with our friends at the Camp Nou football stadium to watch Barcelona play Eibar. The stadium was massive. Messi scored 4 and Arthur scored 1. The score was 5-0 to Barcelona. There were lots of offside goals. There were nearly 68000 people and there was lots of singing and shouting.

Barcelona

We got to Barcelona early yesterday after getting a late flight from Singapore via Doha.

Today we got the metro to the Cathedral where we met up with friends who had come out to see us. Sam and Martha were really happy to see familiar faces after such a long time away.

We had some lunch and then walked over to one of Barcelona’s beaches for a paddle in the water and stone skimming.
