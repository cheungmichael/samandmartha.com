We headed North out of Vancouver along the Sea to Sky Highway and arrived in Whistler 2 hours later. It was a beautiful journey up and a beautiful day to explore the mountain.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I’d been up to Whistler twice previously. The first time was for a few days snowboarding in the winter and the second time was on a day trip in the summer, although I still managed to do some snowboarding up on top.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This time there would be no snowboarding!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We bought our Peak 2 Peak tickets in the village (which was full of mountain bikers and trails coming down) and then took the Whistler Village Gondola up to Roundhouse Lodge (1850m\/6069ft).<\/p>\n\n\n\n
