On our last full day in Hong Kong, we went to Tin Hau Temple which was very close to where we were staying.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It is a traditional Chinese temple and was built in around 1864 to honour the Chinese goddess of the sea, Tin Hau. Inside there were lots of incense coils burning which made the temple smell.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
