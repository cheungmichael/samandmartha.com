When we woke up today we found that at the end of our road there was a festival so we went to have a look. [Note from Mum – we were staying in Little India] There was lots of food and drink, music, bouncy castles, hula hooping and clothes. We ended up staying all day it was so good. In the afternoon there was so much rain, but it was warm so we ran around in it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
