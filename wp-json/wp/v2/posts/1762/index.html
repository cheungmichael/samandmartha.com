Tonight I took another evening stroll and walked along Flinders Street, past the station and towards Southern Cross Station, or Spencer Street Station as I had known it. This was the station I came in to when I used to commute into the centre for work, 11 or so years ago.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I then walked up and along Bourke Street bridge, following my old route to work as I headed towards my workplace at the Telstra Dome (now called Marvel Stadium). I remember the bridge but didn’t recognise the rest of the area so I assume there has been lots of new developments since I was last there. It was good to be back though. I enjoyed living and working in Melbourne all those years ago!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
