Today we left Wanaka and we went to Fox Glacier. It was a long way. We stopped at Lake Hawea and the top of Lake Wanaka to take pictures. Then we stopped for lunch at Blue Pools. It was melted glaciers so it was freezing. We ran in and ran out. We saw people jumping from a high bridge into the water. Lots of sandflies bit mummy. They are called the Dracula of the West Coast.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Next we stopped at Ship Creek having driven through Haast, through the mountains, to the west coast. There were lots of sandflies at Ship Creek. We went on the beach and played cricket. We saw dolphins swimming in the sea.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When we got to Fox Glacier there was a huge trampoline. We made some new friends.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
