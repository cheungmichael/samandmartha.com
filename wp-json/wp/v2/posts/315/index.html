Today we hired some bikes and rode 9km around Stanley Park. Me and daddy got a tandem bike, Martha got her own bike and mummy did as well. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Martha went in a cold water play area. Then we went to a cold swimming pool. It was meant to be heated but it was actually freezing. (Note from parents: it wasn’t actually that cold, we’ve been in much colder!) <\/p>\n\n\n\n
