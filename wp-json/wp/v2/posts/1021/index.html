Today we visited lots of temples:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • Banteay Kdei – good hide and seek spot and big trees<\/li>
  • Sras Srang – it was a calm lake<\/li>
  • Ta Prohm – ancient trees around ancient temples<\/li>
  • Ta Keo – very steep steps, lovely views<\/li>
  • Terrace of the Elephants – beautiful carvings<\/li>
  • Phimeanakas – small but great<\/li>
  • Baphuon – narrow bridge leading to massive temple<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    We did a lot of walking and running around. It was very very very hot. We played hide and seek a lot. There were some steep bits that we went up and down on our bums.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    We had a tuk tuk for the whole day and the driver took us to all the temples. The last few temples were in the Angkor Thom area.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
