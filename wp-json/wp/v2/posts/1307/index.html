In the morning mummy, Martha and I did a walk called the Coalmine Beach Heritage Trail. In the 1930s settlers came to live in this area and would have walked this way to the beach to go fishing.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Then we drove to the Valley of the Giants and did a tree top walk. It was built to protect the tingle trees roots. The walk went up to 40m! The trees were giant. It was sometimes windy so we had to take our caps off. We walked along a metal track and platforms. We did it twice.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We then did a small tour of the forest. We saw lots of big trees. Some had holes in them caused by fires.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
