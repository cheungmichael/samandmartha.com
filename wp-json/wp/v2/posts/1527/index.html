Today we left Queenstown and drove to Arrowtown. We saw where Chinese miners lived when they were mining for gold. We also had a chocolate ice cream which was very nice.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We carried on driving and stopped at Cromwell for lunch. We went to a park and a play area and played in the sand and water.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We then drove on to Wanaka. We had sausages for tea. We then walked along the lake. When we got back to our holiday park it was really late.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
