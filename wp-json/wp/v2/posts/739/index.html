In the morning we caught the bus to see our friend Amandus who we met on the ship to Alaska.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We played football and badminton near his house. Then we went to his sports club and played ping pong. After that I went to his house and held his turtles and they were really fidgety. He also had some big fish, little fish and tadpoles.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Then we went for lunch and we shared a big meaty plate with beef, steak, chicken, pork, chips and gravy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
