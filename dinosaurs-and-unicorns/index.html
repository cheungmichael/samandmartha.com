Dinosaurs and Unicorns

Here are Sam and Martha’s videos from Microsoft’s STEM event “Camp Know Where: Strange-ify Your World” that they attended today.

They were using Surface Pros and Surface Pens, 3D models from the (soon to be retired) Remix3D.com website, the ‘Mixed Reality’ functionality in the ‘Paint 3D’ app and the ‘Photos’ app to create effects and videos from photos.
