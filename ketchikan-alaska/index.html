Ketchikan, Alaska

We arrived at Ketchikan and it was raining a lot. We saw a good lumberjack show and my favourite bit was the axe throwing and the tree climbing. After that we saw some totem poles and we also saw lots of salmon swimming up a stream.

Then we collected MORE free jewellery like pendants, necklaces, bracelets and charms.
