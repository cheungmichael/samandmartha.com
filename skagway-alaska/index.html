Skagway, Alaska

Today we went to Skagway.

First we stroked sled dogs and puppies! They were really cute, fluffy and soft.

Next we went in to a room that was 40 degrees below zero and it was freezing!

Then we went gold panning. I got gold that was worth $14.13. I got it put in to a necklace.

Next we got some more free jewellery.

(Note from Mike – I went on a “Guided Photography Adventure” later on with a small group of like-minded individuals and we managed to explore some areas around Skagway, including driving up the Klondike Highway and crossing the border into Canada and back.)
